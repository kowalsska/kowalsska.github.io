About

Hi, I’m Magda. I created this website as a personal project to learn something new and share with people projects I work on and things I do. I’m currently based in San Francisco, CA where I work as a software engineer at Getaround, a company that empowers people to carshare everywhere. In my free time I often rent Getaround cars to go climbing, cycling or surfing around California. Before moving to US, I worked remotely as a full stack engineer at OhmConnect while driving my van across Europe to Morocco. Hope you find my website interesting.
